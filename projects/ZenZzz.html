Project Overview

The ZenZzz project addresses the critical yet often overlooked domain of sleep health, with a particular focus on university students. Recognizing the challenges these students face in balancing academic and social commitments, our goal is to integrate regular, healthy sleep patterns into their daily routines. ZenZzz, a mobile application is designed to offer a comprehensive sleep health management solution. The app will enable users to track, analyze, and improve their sleep routines through user-friendly interfaces and personalized feedback. By promoting healthy sleep habits, ZenZzz aims to support university students in overcoming sleep issues and enhancing their overall well-being.

UX Research

Students' Sleep Challenges

Academic Pressure

University students often face intense academic demands, including exams, assignments, and projects, which can lead to late-night study sessions and irregular sleep schedules.

Long Screen Time

Excessive use of electronic devices, especially before bedtime, can interfere with the ability to fall asleep and maintain a regular sleep pattern.

Lifestyle Choices

Poor lifestyle habits, such as consuming caffeine or alcohol, and irregular eating patterns, negatively impacts sleep quality and consistency.

Mental Issues

Depression, anxiety disorders, and other mental health challenges impact sleep quality.

Medication

Some medicines have side effects that can lead to difficulties with sleeping.

Interview

Based on the survey results, we selected interview participants who had sleep problems and experienced different factors contributing to poor sleep quality. We particularly included those who had used sleep tracking apps because their experiences could provide valuable insights into the effectiveness and limitations of existing solutions. This allowed us to investigate various perspectives and reasons behind their sleep issues.

Feature Roadmap

Feature Description Feature Breakdown
P1: Must-have Home Default page with the presentation of major features. Presentation and entry for reward point, sleep duration and setting, recommendation.
Sleep tracking activation Start to track sleeping time and record any sleep factor. Contain sleep factors such as eating habits, emotion, activities.
Sleep report Provide comprehensive insights into users’ sleep patterns and quality. Options for daily, weekly, and monthly report overviews.
Rewards system Encourage users to maintain healthy sleep habits by offering rewards. Points, rewards, redeem process
Habits/Emotion records Sleep tracking activation to record any sleep factors possibly affecting users’ sleep quality. Daily logs of emotions and habits.
Signup/Login Set up account to identify their data. Secure user authentication, data management and privacy.
Sleep tips content Provide helpful sleep information. Multimedia content on sleep practices
Settings Allow users to customize their app experience. Bedtime & Alarm setting, sleep recording history, help support.
P2: Nice to have Sleep records history View past sleep data and trends over time. Accessible archives of all recorded sleep data.
Advisors Professional advice for better sleep management. Connect with sleep experts and coaches
Customized alarm setting Set alarms tailored to individual sleep patterns and preferences. Gradual wake-up alarms, multiple alarms.
P3: Surprising and delightful Recommendations Personalized suggestions based on sleep data. Advice on improving sleep quality.
Bookmarks Save favorite sleep tips and articles for quick access. Easy bookmarking and retrieval system.
Notification Alerts and reminders design. Push notifications and reminders.
P4: Can come later Integration with wearable devices. Synchronize data from smartwatches and other wearables. Seamless data import from external devices.
Integration with mobile phones’ built-in health apps Leverage existing health data from phones. Data sharing with phone’s health apps (e.g., Apple Health, Google Fit).
Interactive user guide for novice users Provide a walkthrough and tips for new users. Step-by-step tutorial and FAQs.
