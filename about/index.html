About Me

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Literature, and stepping into a software product management role right after graduation was an unconventional choice for someone from my field. However, my passion for creating impactful products drove me to embrace the challenge and pursue the path of product management. I've always been fascinated by human behavior and thrive in cross-team collaborations. Of course, working in product management came with its challenges—sometimes dealing with the “dirty things”, but over the course of 3 years, I've developed invaluable communication skills, especially learning how to negotiate and find win-win solutions for all parties involved.

2021 marked a turning point for me when I discovered UI/UX design. This new field ignited a passion I didn't expect, and I quickly dove into the Google UX course. From there, I had the opportunity to lead internal UI/UX projects, redesign products, and even worked on a side project where I revamped Taiwan's second-largest convenience store app. The more I immersed myself in this world, the clearer it became that this was the direction I wanted to pursue.

In 2023, I made a decisive move to focus on Interaction design and joined University of Queensland. This experience has been incredibly inspiring—both in terms of design and technical growth. At UQ, I've explored XR design, developed a VR 3D game, worked on 6 UI/UX design projects, and even took on front-end web development. With each new challenge, my passion for crafting meaningful digital solutions with a user-centered approach only grows stronger.
